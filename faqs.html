What do you need from me in order to start working?

In order to get started all, we need is certificate of insurance , your W9 & MC number

Will you provide me with a invoice?

Yes, Our invoices are very detailed and they have details of the loads that you accepted and rejected with a date.

What is it that i need to do to sign up with you and get started?

Here are the following documents you need to get started • Certificate of Liability Insurance • Copy of Carrier’s Operating Authority (MC Authority Permit) • Copy of CDL (for each driver being dispatched) • Signed W-9

How much percent of the payload will be mine?

Payload will be 100% yours, and there are certainly no forced dispatch.

Do I need to setup paperwork to get started?

No, Let’s take care of all the the paperwork for you. As we mentioned earlier, we only need your certificate of insurance, and your W9 & MC number to start off.

What are the Payment methods so I can send you money?

We accept all Credit Cards, Zelle App or Cash App. Moreover, you can transfer funds to our USA bank account too.

Are there any discounts for 1 or more trucks?

If you refer us to any carrier, we can offer you exceptional discounts. Contact us at +1 (302) 208-7771.

Do I need to pay before I get the service or after?

You do not need to pay any upfront fee. We’ll charge you every weekend after serving you.

I don’t have my Motor Carrier Authority. Do you provide your Authority to run Under?

As an independent dispatch firm, we do not own any MC authority. We will work on your MC so that you can pay directly from the broker.

What information does my driver receive about dispatch?

Drivers are given complete load information, including addresses, pick-up location, commodity specification, and special instructions.

What happens if dispatch details change?

Our dispatchers are always in touch with both brokers and drivers, so in case of any change or cancellation, we will notify them immediately.
