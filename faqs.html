Frequently Asked Questions

What Is Truck Dispatch Service?

Organizing pickups, scheduling deliveries, consolidating cargo, enforcing safety regulations, monitoring delivery fleets, maintaining records, and other tasks are also included in truck dispatching. It also involves coordinating and managing freight transit between destinations.

What Documents Do I Need To Use Dispatch Services?

Certificate of MC authority SSN or Verified EIN from IRS Certificate of Insurance Signed Dispatch – Carrier Agreement Online Identity Verifications UIIA, TWIC & SCAC ,Driving License Front & Back

How Can I Get Onboard For Independent Dispatch Services?

Collect all the required documents and send them to Fleet Support Signed the Dispatch-Carrier agreements Get the Identity Verification Done

How will I Be Charged For Dispatch Services?

Dispatch Services can be charged on a percentage basis per the agreement Signed by the Carrier and Dispatcher as per agreed terms and Conditions. We accept bi-weekly (15th of the month) and monthly payments (1st of the month).

How Do I Get Paid?

Once the Load gets completed, the dispatcher will collect the paperwork from the driver and hand it over to the accounting team. The accounts team will invoice and submit to the broker along with paperwork and rate confirmations. The customer will make payment directly into the Carrier’s bank accounts on the invoice’s due date.

What If I Am Unable To Cover The Load?

Carrier Can Cancel or reschedule the load as we do not allow force dispatch. All the loads are being booked with 100% driver consent only.

Do You Accept New MC?

Yes, We offer Dispatch Services to New MC Operators as well. We have moved many trucks with Brand New Operating Authority. The carrier needs the load! We need business. Its simple!!!
