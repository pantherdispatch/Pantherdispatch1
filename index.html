Getting Started With Jaguar Dispatch Services

As we believe in the absolutely no-nonsense policy, our truck dispatch services setup packet is straightforward. Once you’re ready to start working with us, we’ll send you a link to the carrier application. When all the paperwork is completed, you’ll be instantly introduced to your personal dispatcher so you can start working immediately. Jaguar Dispatch Services is a reputed and reliable dispatch service company with great relationships with carriers and clients we work with. These values make us stand out from the rest. Here is what you need to sign up:

  • Signed Truck Dispatch Service Agreement
  • Factoring Information
  • General Power Of Attorney (for contacting brokers on your behalf)
  • Copy Of Your MC Authority Letter
  • Copy Of The Insurance Certificate
  • Driver’s Contact Info
  • Copy Of W-9 Form
